|-----------|------------|---------|
| Standard Rate | 20% | Most goods & services |

## VAT Registration Thresholds

- 📈 Mandatory Registration: £90,000 turnover
- 📉 Voluntary Registration: Below £90,000
- ❌ Deregistration Threshold: £88,000

## Flat Rate Scheme

- Simplified VAT for small businesses
- Pay fixed % of gross turnover
- No need to track input VAT separately
- Available if turnover under £150,000
